Who We Are

At Benshada Place, we enable African fashion houses and businesses extend their market reach across borders; enabling buyers to source their products with ease, convenience and speed.


What We Do

We believe in the ease of doing business within your environment, and afar off.
We make this possible by;

  1. helping established African fashion houses and businesses with their products expansion, manufacturing and branding.
    That way, maintaining quality in terms of beauty and standards.
  2. helping new African fashion house and business owners plan, start and effortlessly run their fashion labels.
    That way, ensuring a smooth work flow, while they focus on customers, marketing and sales.
  3. ensuring quality and timely delivery no matter the order quantity.
